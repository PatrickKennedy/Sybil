The Low Down

  • Worlds provide a convenient way of tying profiles with common backgrounds together.
  • A few examples include:
    - Fleshing out the world several of your characters are in.
    - Providing a central reposity for Role Playing characters.
    - Creating "Theme" worlds for people to make up characters for (See Springfield).

Available Options

  • Public/Private
    - When Public the world can viewed by anyone.
    - When Private the world can only be viewed by members.
  • Open/Closed
    - When Open anyone who can view the world is allowed to add profiles.
    - When Closed only members can add profiles.
  • Markup
    Sybil supports two markup languages. Which you use is entirely up to you.
    - Textile is simpler and easier to understand
    - reStructuredText is better suited for developers who already use it.
